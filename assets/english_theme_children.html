Grandpa's Surprise Visit

Last summer, my grandfather drove all the way from Zagora to visit us in Casablanca. When he came in, he brought a huge basket of dates. "I knew you would love these!" he said. My little sister felt so excited she ran around the house. That evening, we all ate couscous together and told stories. Grandpa taught me how to play chess. I thought it was difficult at first, but then I became better. When he left, we all wept, but he promised to return soon.

The Lost Camel of Merzouga

While visiting Merzouga, my cousin Ahmed rode a camel for the first time. Suddenly, the camel broke its rope and ran away! We spent hours searching. The sun shone brightly and we grew tired. Just as we thought to give up, Ahmed heard a noise. Behind a dune, the camel stood calmly eating grass! We led it back safely. That night, we all slept like babies.
